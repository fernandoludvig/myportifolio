From enhancing existing web applications to creating new ones from scratch, I have been actively engaged in the entire development lifecycle. Throughout my internship, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with experienced professionals who have guided me in honing my coding skills and improving my problem-solving abilities. This exposure to the backend aspects of web development has been instrumental in broadening my skill set and solidifying my passion for this field.

In addition to technical skills, my time at Florianopolis City Hall has also taught me the importance of effective communication and collaboration. Working in a team environment, I have learned to effectively communicate ideas, contribute to discussions, and work towards common goals. These interpersonal skills have been vital in fostering a positive and productive work environment.

Overall, my experience as an intern at Florianopolis City Hall has been incredibly enriching. It has provided me with valuable exposure to PHP, CSS, and HTML development, as well as the opportunity to work with industry professionals. This internship has not only expanded my technical abilities but also deepened my passion for web development and reinforced my commitment to pursuing a career in this dynamic field.
